East Valley Simplex Net (EVSN)

What:

An amateur radio simplex net geared toward folks in the East San Fernando Valley: Burbank, North Hollywood, Glendale if we can pull it off, etc.). Informal, so come and go as you please

When / Where:

1st and 3rd Sunday of the month @ 9pm, 144.250 MHz
Mode: FM   PL: None

Why:

To have fun, share advice, and get to know our ham neighbors
