East Valley Simplex Round Table (EVSRT)

What

An informal round table geared toward amateur radio operators in the East San Fernando Valley: Burbank, North Hollywood, Glendale, etc.), but everyone is welcome! Informal, so come and go as you please. Especially welcoming to new hams

Why

To have fun, share advice, and get to know our ham neighbors

When / Where

1st and 3rd Sunday of the month @ 9pm, 146.450 MHz
(+ or - 0.02 MHz if frequency is in use)
Mode: FM   PL: None
